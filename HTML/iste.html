Origin Story

Legacy of Excellence

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading national non-profit society for the welfare of the technical education system in India. Originally stemming from the Association of Principals of Technical Institutions (APTI) established in 1941, it has transformed into a massive network bridging the gap between academia, industry, and society.
